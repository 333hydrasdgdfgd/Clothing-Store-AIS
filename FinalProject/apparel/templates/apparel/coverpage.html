What it does

Everything You Need

From managing products to tracking every sale and purchase, Aura OS covers all operations of your apparel business.

👕

Product Management

Organize apparel by category, brand, size, color, and material. Set unit costs with automatic 5% markup for selling price.

📊

Sales Orders

Record walk-in and online transactions. Accept cash, card, or e-wallet payments. Apply item and order-level discounts.

🚚

Purchase Orders

Create purchase orders per supplier. Add multiple products, apply discounts, and track order status from pending to received.

🏭

Supplier Management

Maintain supplier records and link brands to suppliers. Filter products by supplier when creating purchase orders.

👤

Customer Records

Track customer details and view their complete sales history. All transactions are automatically linked to the customer.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Each employee only sees what they need. Admins get full access while staff see only their relevant sections.
